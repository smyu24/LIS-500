Welcome to our website! This site explores power relations in the tech industry, implicit bias, and ways to drive fairness by race, class, and gender.

About Implicit Bias

Implicit bias refers to the attitudes or stereotypes that affect our understanding, actions, and decisions unconsciously. These biases shape how people interact with technology and the workplace.
