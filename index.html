Welcome!

Welcome to our website! This site explores power relations in the tech industry, Implicit Bias, and how to drive fairness by race, class, and gender. Here you will find an introduction to me, a resource guide, and an introduction to a "tech hero" who is challenging the status quo and pushing for fairness in technology.

We hope this site inspires you to explore equity and diversity in the world of technology!
