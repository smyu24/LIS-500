Meet Constanza Gomez

Constanza Gomez is the co-founder and CEO of Sortile, a pioneering tech company revolutionizing textile recycling. She stands at the forefront of sustainability and artificial intelligence, striving to reduce textile waste through innovative sorting technology.

Innovating for Sustainability

Sortile originated as a research initiative at Columbia University, aiming to improve textile sorting and recycling methods. With the backing of the Morgan Stanley Inclusive & Sustainable Ventures Lab (MSISVL), Gomez transformed it into a scalable business focused on AI-driven recycling solutions.

Since its expansion, Sortile has partnered with multiple organizations, diverting over 100 tons of textiles from landfills while enhancing circular economy initiatives.

Meet Stephanie Lampkin

Stephanie Lampkin is a software engineer and entrepreneur dedicated to combating bias in the tech industry.

After experiencing firsthand the challenges of being overlooked for technical roles, she founded Blendoor, an app designed to anonymize job applicants' profiles to reduce bias in hiring. Blendoor allows job seekers to upload resumes while concealing details such as age, gender, and race, promoting equal opportunities for all applicants.

Innovating for Sustainability

Stephanie Lampkin innovates for sustainability by promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in tech. Through Blendoor and other initiatives, she addresses bias in hiring and advocates for equitable opportunities and compensation.

Impact & Recognition

Stephanie Lampkin has made a significant impact by challenging bias in the tech industry. Through Blendoor, she has helped make hiring more equitable by anonymizing resumes to reduce discrimination.

Her work has been widely recognized, earning her accolades like being named to Forbes' 30 Under 30 and recognized by Fast Company for her innovation in diversity and inclusion. Lampkin's leadership has empowered underrepresented groups in tech, particularly women and people of color, and her advocacy for fair pay and equal opportunities continues to inspire industry change.
