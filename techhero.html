Meet Constanza Gomez

Constanza Gomez is the co-founder and CEO of Sortile, a groundbreaking tech startup focused on transforming textile recycling. She is a leader in the intersection of sustainability and technology, working to reduce landfill waste through AI-driven sorting systems.

Innovating for Sustainability

Sortile was originally developed at Columbia University as a research tool for studying textile sorting and collection. With support from the Morgan Stanley Inclusive & Sustainable Ventures Lab (MSISVL), Gomez and her team pivoted their focus to scalable AI-driven textile recycling.

Since joining MSISVL, Sortile has expanded from a single business to partnering with over five off-takers, processing over 100 tons of textiles.

Impact & Recognition

Through mentorship and funding from MSISVL, Gomez has been able to refine her business strategy, improve outreach, and contribute to a more sustainable world. Her leadership exemplifies how technology can drive positive change in environmental and social sustainability.
