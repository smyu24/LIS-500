Meet Constanza Gomez

Constanza Gomez is the co-founder and CEO of Sortile, a pioneering tech company revolutionizing textile recycling. She stands at the forefront of sustainability and artificial intelligence, striving to reduce textile waste through innovative sorting technology.

Innovating for Sustainability

Sortile originated as a research initiative at Columbia University, aiming to improve textile sorting and recycling methods. With the backing of the Morgan Stanley Inclusive & Sustainable Ventures Lab (MSISVL), Gomez transformed it into a scalable business focused on AI-driven recycling solutions.

Since its expansion, Sortile has partnered with multiple organizations, diverting over 100 tons of textiles from landfills while enhancing circular economy initiatives.
