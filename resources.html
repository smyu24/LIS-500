Understanding Implicit Bias

Implicit bias refers to attitudes or stereotypes that affect our understanding, actions, and decisions in an unconscious manner. These biases can impact various aspects of life, including education, employment, and healthcare.

Key Topics

  • Implicit Bias Test (IBT): Learn how implicit biases are measured and what they reveal about our subconscious attitudes.
  • Intersectionality: Explore how overlapping social identities, such as race, gender, and class, shape individual experiences.
  • Effects of Implicit Bias: Understand how biases influence decision-making in institutions and everyday interactions.

Recommended Resources

  • Learning for Justice - Articles and resources on reducing bias and promoting equity.
  • American Psychological Association - Implicit Bias - Insights into how bias affects society.
  • National Equity Project - Strategies for addressing and mitigating bias.
